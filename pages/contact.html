Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and scheduling with us

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly at 987-6543, or sending an email to info@lakenormandogtraining.com. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to set up a time that works for you.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, we offer a free initial consultation to discuss your dog's needs and determine the best training approach. This can be done over the phone or in person, depending on your preference.

How far in advance should I book training sessions?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for private sessions and 2-3 weeks for group classes, as our schedule tends to fill up quickly, especially during peak seasons.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via Zoom or FaceTime for clients who prefer this option or live just outside our service area. This can be a great way to get started before in-person training.
