Basic Obedience Training

Our Basic Obedience Training program is designed to teach your dog essential commands and proper manners, creating a well-behaved companion that responds reliably to your cues. This foundational training is perfect for dogs of all ages and breeds who need to learn or refresh their basic skills.

Prevent your dog from picking up harmful items and release objects on command

Teach your dog to greet people and other dogs calmly without jumping

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dog training services

How do I know which training program is right for my dog?

We recommend starting with a free consultation where we can assess your dog's needs, discuss your goals, and recommend the most appropriate program. Every dog is unique, and we'll help you choose the option that best addresses your specific situation.

How long does it take to see results?

Many clients see initial improvements within the first few sessions, but lasting behavior change typically takes 4-8 weeks of consistent practice. The timeline depends on the specific behavior, your dog's temperament, and your commitment to practicing between sessions. Complex behavioral issues may require longer-term training.

Do you use treats in training?

Yes, we use positive reinforcement methods that include treats, praise, play, and other rewards. Treats are especially helpful in the early stages of training to establish behaviors. As training progresses, we gradually reduce the frequency of treats while maintaining the desired behaviors.

Is my dog too old to train?

No! The saying "you can't teach an old dog new tricks" is a myth. Dogs of any age can learn new behaviors and modify existing ones. While puppies may learn some things more quickly, adult and senior dogs often have longer attention spans and can make excellent students.

What training equipment do you use?

We primarily use flat collars, harnesses, and 6-foot leashes for most training. We do not use or recommend shock collars, prong collars, or other aversive tools. We'll help you select the most appropriate equipment for your specific dog and training goals.

Do you offer any guarantees?

While we can't guarantee specific results since they depend on many factors including owner follow-through, we do guarantee our commitment to you and your dog. If you're not seeing progress, we'll adjust our approach or recommend alternative solutions to help you reach your goals.
