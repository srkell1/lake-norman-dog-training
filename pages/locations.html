Our Service Area

Lake Norman Dog Training proudly serves the entire Lake Norman region and surrounding communities

Interactive Map of Lake Norman Service Area

Lake Norman Dog Training provides professional dog training services within a 20-mile radius of Lake Norman, covering all the major communities in the area. Whether you're in a lakefront property or a neighborhood further inland, we're here to help with all your dog training needs.

We offer both in-home training sessions where we come to you, as well as training at our partner facilities throughout the region. Our flexible scheduling and multiple locations ensure that quality dog training is accessible to everyone in the Lake Norman area.

Our Training Facilities

In addition to in-home training, we offer sessions at these partner locations

Lake Norman Dog Training Center

Mooresville, NC

Our main training facility offers both indoor and outdoor training areas, allowing for year-round training regardless of weather conditions. The center features:

  • Climate-controlled indoor training area
  • Fenced outdoor training yard
  • Agility equipment for confidence building
  • Specialized training props and obstacles
  • Comfortable waiting area for clients

This location hosts our group classes, puppy socialization sessions, and is available for private training appointments.

Schedule Training at This Location

Lakeside Pet Resort

Cornelius, NC

Our partnership with Lakeside Pet Resort allows us to offer training in conjunction with boarding services. This is ideal for:

  • Board and train programs
  • Training while you're on vacation
  • Day training while you're at work
  • Specialized water and boat training (seasonal)

The resort's lakefront location provides a unique opportunity for dogs to practice skills in a water environment, perfect for families with boats who want their dogs to be comfortable and safe on the water.

Schedule Training at This Location

Community Parks

Various Locations

We conduct training sessions at several community parks throughout the Lake Norman area. These real-world environments are excellent for:

  • Practicing skills with real-life distractions
  • Leash training in public settings
  • Socialization with controlled exposure to other people and dogs
  • Building confidence in various environments

Park locations include Bailey Road Park (Cornelius), Roosevelt Wilson Park (Davidson), Liberty Park (Mooresville), and North Mecklenburg Park (Huntersville).

Not in Our Service Area?

Options for dog owners outside our 20-mile radius

If you're located outside our primary service area, we still have options to help you with your dog training needs:

Board & Train Programs

Your dog stays at our facility for intensive training, and we provide detailed handover sessions when you pick up your dog.

Virtual Consultations

We offer video consultations to provide guidance and training plans you can implement at home.

Extended Travel Fee

For locations just beyond our standard service area, we may be able to accommodate with an additional travel fee.

Referral Network

We maintain relationships with qualified trainers throughout North Carolina and can provide referrals to trusted colleagues in your area.

Contact us to discuss your location and needs, and we'll help find the best solution for you and your dog.
